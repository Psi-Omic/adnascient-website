U-Check

TRL 2

The first end-to-end health monitoring system — a multiplexed test strip that detects CRP, ferritin, and haemoglobin from a finger-prick blood sample, paired with a smartphone app that delivers a clear, actionable wellness report.

Three biomarkers. One strip. One report.
Clear, actionable health reports — not just raw numbers.
Designed for preventive, longitudinal monitoring.
Works with finger-prick and menstrual blood samples.
