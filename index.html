About Us

Built by scientists.

U-Check

TRL 2

A multiplexed lateral flow immunoassay strip that detects CRP, ferritin, and haemoglobin simultaneously from a finger-prick blood sample — paired with a smartphone app that delivers computational biology-driven wellness reports.

Three biomarkers. One strip. Two minutes.
Intelligent health reports — not just raw numbers.
Designed for preventive, longitudinal monitoring.
Works with finger-prick and menstrual blood samples.
